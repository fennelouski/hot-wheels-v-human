- `DriveSystem.swift` тАФ RealityKit `System` with TWO propulsion modes, chosen per car by its physics body mode (set at spawn from `RaceTuning.railPinned`, default ON; Test Mode has the A/B toggle):
  - **Rail mode** (kinematic, default): the car is pinned to its lane spline and can never leave the track. `railStep` (pure, unit-tested in `RailFollowerTests`) integrates one scalar speed (drive тИТ drag тИТ slope gravity + loop motor + boost), advances an arc-length position, and PLACES the car at `spline(d) + lateral┬╖drift + up┬╖rideHeight`, oriented by the track frame (`up = tangent ├Ч lateral` тАФ loops roll the car upside down correctly). Corners read as stat-driven drift (`driftFactor`/`tireGripFactor` тЖТ lateral slide + slip-angle yaw, capped inside the rails); crests and ramp lips launch a ballistic arc that stays above the lane line and lands where the arc meets the bed тАФ jump length falls out of speed/boost/stats. A kid-first `minCrawlSpeed` floor means no car is ever stranded; rail cars skip all destruction checks (they can't fall, wedge, or flip).
  - **Chaos mode** (dynamic): the original force physics тАФ drive force along the tangent, PD steering, soft magnet, recovery reel-in, unstick shove (flying off IS the game). Everything below about catapults/rescues applies to this mode.
- `RaceRulesSystem.swift` тАФ checkpoint crossing (piece boundaries), lap/finish detection, destruction checks per RaceTuning, debris spawn (`debris-*` models, random impulses, 3 s despawn), life decrement, respawn at last checkpoint after delay, win/lose determination. - `RaceCoordinator.swift` тАФ host-side state machine `lobby тЖТ collectingDesigns тЖТ buildingTrack тЖТ countdown тЖТ racing тЖТ paused/finished тЖТ results`. Owns transport events in, snapshots out @10 Hz, boost validation (server-side meter), AI opponent driver.
- `AIBoostPolicy.swift` тАФ easy/medium/hard boost timing per PRD ┬з6.4 (reads upcoming piece types from the spline/piece map).
- `PhysicsEvents.swift` тАФ collision-event subscription тЖТ crash SFX triggers + reaction-cam event feed.
